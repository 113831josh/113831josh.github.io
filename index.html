What is inside the black box?

Claim

I think there is nothing in the Black Box. Think of it like a water bottle with a hole in it. If you fill it up to the line and keep going, it will eventually just let everything go back out through the hole and the water line will be back at the line right above the hole.

Evidence

The mystery black box worked oddly. Water went in, paused, then more water, then all of the water came out. Our model's key component was the bucket with the looped tube; water would go in, pause, more water goes in, and then it all comes out at the same time. However, when we began to conduct experiments, the water began to leak out the bottom. We did not know if the bucket was poorly made or if this just was not the answer. We also got the majority of the same numbers for each trial, the first, no water came out, then for the rest the amount put in came out.
